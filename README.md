# Insurance-data-Analysis
To analyze customer policy data, claim status, premium distribution, and policy activity to help PRISM Insurance Pvt. Ltd. understand business performance, customer behavior, and financial risk.



# Project Objective
The objective of this project is to evaluate key aspects of insurance operations by analyzing customer demographics, policy activity, premium contributions, claim patterns, and financial performance. This analysis aims to uncover insights that help the company optimize policy offerings, improve claim processing efficiency, reduce financial risk, and enhance customer satisfaction.

# Questions(KPIs)
- Total Claim Amount
- Total Coverage Amount
- Total Premium Amount
- Customer Gender Count (Male / Female)
- Premium Amount by Policy Type
- Active vs Inactive Policy Count
- Number of Claims by Status (Rejected, Settled, Pending)
- Claim Amount by Age Group
- Policy-wise Pending & Rejected Claim Summary
  
  
# Process
- Imported raw data containing customer details, policies, premiums, and claims.
- Cleaned and formatted the data by fixing errors, removing duplicates, and organizing categories.
- Performed data modeling to link customer, policy, and claim information.
- Developed KPIs such as claim amount, premium amount, active policies, and claim status counts.
- Designed dashboards with charts, slicers, and tables for better understanding of operations.
- Analyzed insights to identify trends in claims, policy activity, age groups, and policy types.
